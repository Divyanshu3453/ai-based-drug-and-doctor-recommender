What is CuraPath?

CuraPath is an AI medical assistant that helps you understand your symptoms, identify possible conditions, find the right specialists nearby, and learn about relevant drug formulations — all in one place. Designed as a first step, not a replacement for professional care.

Key features

Symptom analysis

Text or image input with AI-powered condition matching and probability scores

Doctor matching

Find the right specialist near you with ratings, experience and availability

Drug insights

Drug class, mechanism, dosage, formulation and contraindication reference

History tracking

Revisit past consultations within your current session
